HOW TO DO I DELETE MY ACCOUNT?

To delete your account and all the information we have on you, do the following:

  • Open the Flobux App.
  • Sign in to Flobux.
  • Open the Menu by pressing the top left hamburger menu button.
  • Click on the 'Settings' option.
  • Click on the 'Data' menu option.
  • Click on 'Delete All Data (Local & Cloud)' menu option.
  • Click 'Delete' on the 'Are you sure?' pop up.

If you run into any issues, please email flobuxapp@gmail.com to ensure all your data is successfully deleted.

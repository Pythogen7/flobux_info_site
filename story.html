Flobux's Story

Let me begin by introducing myself. My name is Evan Carr, and I am the creator of Flobux. Flobux is not for profit.

  • Vision Our vision is to create a world where every individual has the tools to achieve financial peace, responsibility and prosperity.
  • Mission Our mission is to provide an accessible and user-friendly budgeting tool to promote financial peace, responsibility and prosperity.

Where does Flobux's story begin?

As the world increasingly adopts digital wallets, I recognized a need for a centralized platform to track and manage my finances. Existing solutions were either overly complex or over simplified and often unintuitive, inspiring me to develop my own solution. A very simple version of what would become Flobux was born.

I discovered many of my friends were struggling with financial organization, and I began brainstorming what it might look like to bring Flobux into a fully developed software for everyone to use.

Initially intended for my personal use, Flobux quickly evolved into a mission-driven initiative upon recognizing the widespread need for a more accessible and user-friendly budgeting tool.

Flobux is not aimed for profit. Any payments made towards additional links are directed towards supporting other users who utilize the free version of Flobux.
